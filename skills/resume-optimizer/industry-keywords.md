## Compliance Frameworks

**Information Security:**
- NIST 800-53 (Rev 4, Rev 5)
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
- COBIT
- CRI Profile
- NIST AI RMF

**Industry-Specific:**
- SOC 2 Type II (AICPA)
- SOX ITGC (IT General Controls)
- PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry)
- GDPR (EU Data Protection)
- DORA (Digital Operational Resilience Act)

**Cloud Security:**
- CSA CCM (Cloud Controls Matrix)
- AWS Security Best Practices
- GCP Security Command Center

**Financial Services:**
- GLBA (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act)
- FFIEC (Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council)Handbook
- OCC Heightened Standards
- MAS TRM (Monetary Authority of Singapore Technology Risk Management)

## Common GRC Terms

**Risk Management:**
- Risk assessment, Risk register, Risk appetite, Risk tolerance
- Threat modeling, Vulnerability management
- Third-party risk management (TPRM)
- Vendor risk assessment
- Application and Infrastructure Risk Assessment

**Audit & Compliance:**
- Control testing, Control design, Control effectiveness
- Audit evidence, Audit findings, Remediation
- Continuous monitoring, Automated testing
- Compliance attestation, Regulatory examination

**Governance:**
- Policy framework, Standards, Procedures
- Control framework, Control environment
- Governance committee, Risk committee
- Compliance reporting, Board reporting

## Key Metrics to Quantify

- Control improvements
- Risk reduction
- Regulatory findings reduced (count or %)
- Number of regulatory examinations supported
- Team size managed
- Number of business units/applications in scope
- Remediation timelines adhered to
- Time saved through process improvements (audit deleivery reduced by 20%)

## Role-Specific Language

**For GRC/Compliance roles emphasize:**
- Frameworks by exact name (NIST 800-53 Rev 5, SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001)
- Regulatory relationships (FRB, OCC, MAS, PRA, FINRA)
- Quantified compliance metrics (controls managed, findings reduced, audit timelines)
- Stakeholder management (number of business units, regulatory agencies, audit partners)

**For Product Management pivots:**
- Customer-centric language (user research, customer feedback, market validation)
- Metrics (DAU/MAU, conversion rates, adoption rates)
- Cross-functional collaboration (engineering, design, sales)
- Delivery (sprints, releases, features shipped)
